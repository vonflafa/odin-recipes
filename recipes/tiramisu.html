How to make a perfect tiramisu

The best tiramisu of the world

Tiramisu is one of the foundamental recipe every cooker should know.

Here we are going to give you the best recipe to prepare a perfect traditional tiramisu.

Cook it and let us know if it is or not the best tiramisu you've ever tasted!

Ingredients:

  • 750g Mascarpone cheese
  • 250g Ladyfingers
  • 300g Coffee
  • 260g Eggs
  • 100g Sugar
  • Small amount of cocoa powder

  1. Make the coffee

    Prepare the coffee using a moka pot, you have to obtain about 300g.

    Let it cool down in a low profile bowl, possibly a wide one.

  2. Make the cream

    Separate very carefully the yolks from the whites.

    Beat the yolks with half the sugar, until the mixture is foamy and pale.

    Slightly add the mascarpone cheese and keep beating the mixture until thickened.

    3. Beat the egg whites

    Beat the egg whites until they get very foamy.

    Slowly add the rest of the sugar and keep beating.

  3. Mix the creams

    Put the whites mixture over the mascarpone one and gently mix the creams.

  4. Assemble the tiramisu

    Pour each ladyfinger into the coffee and let them soak for 2-3 seconds.

    Create a first layer of soaked ladyfinger on the bottom of a baking dish.

    Spread 1/3 of the cream over the ladyfingers.

    Repeat in the same order.

    Dust with cocoa powder.
