How to make a Caesar salad from scratch!

Caesar salad? It's never been so easy!

Believe it or not, creating a Caesar salad from-scratch is actually really easy.

All you'll need is romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing.

Prepare it and let us know if it is or not the best Caesar salad you've ever had!

Ingredients:

  • 60g Melted butter
  • 140g Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 15g Capers
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 15g Dijon mustard
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5g Worcestershire sauce
  • 20g Lemon juice
  • 25g Parmesan cheese

    How to Make Homemade Croutons

  1. Start with crusty bread (day-old bread is even better), and cut it into cubes.
  2. Add your bread cubes to a baking sheet and coat them in a mixture of:

    • melted butter
    • 60g extra-virgin olive oil
    • two cloves of minced garlic
    • salt
    • pepper

  3. Toss the bread to evenly coat each cube and bake for 15 minutes in an oven preheated to 180°C.

    4. How to Make Homemade Caesar Dressing

  4. Start by making a paste with two cloves of garlic and capers.
  5. Add the Dijon mustard, the egg yolk, the Worcestershire sauce, and the lemon juice.
  6. Mix those ingredients together until combined.
  7. Whisk in 80g of extra-virgin olive oil — whisking is key to fully incorporate the oil into the dressing.
  8. Grate in the fresh Parmesan cheese and add some cracked pepper.
  9. Mix the dressing until smooth and emulsified, then set it aside until needed.

    10. How to Assemble Caesar Salad

  10. Chop and wash your romaine lettuce.

    Make sure the lettuce is completely dry before adding the dressing, or else the dressing will slide right off.

    This would be a good time to use a salad spinner, or you can simply dry the leaves with paper towels.

  11. Add your lettuce, croutons, dressing, and some shaved Parmesan cheese to a bowl.
  12. Toss the salad to coat it in dressing and serve.
  13. Enjoy your fresh and homemade Caesar salad!
