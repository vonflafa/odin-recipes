How to make a perfect lasagna

The best lasagna of the world

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Cook it and let us know if it is or not the best lasagna you've ever tasted!

Ingredients:

  • 800g Pasta sheets
  • 500g Minced beef
  • 150g Bacon
  • 50g Carrots
  • 50g Celery
  • 50g Onions
  • 100g Red wine
  • 300g Tomato sauce
  • 750g Béchamel
  • 270g Parmesan cheese
  • A small amount of butter

    The bolognese sauce

  1. Chop the bacon very well.
  2. Separately chop finely carrots, onions and celery.
  3. In a saucepan let the bacon brown for a few minutes.
  4. Add the chopped vegetables and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  5. CAdd the minced meat, raise the heat and let the meat brown.
  6. Deglaze with the red wine and let it evaporate completely.
  7. Add the tomato sauce.
  8. Add 1 litre of water and cover it with a lid and lower the heat.
  9. Check and stir from time to time, if necessary add more water.
  10. Let it cook for at least 2 hours.

    11. How to Layer Lasagna

  11. Take a large pan (30x20cm) and butter the bottom.
  12. Add a thin layer of béchamel and one of ragù.
  13. Add the grated parmesan.
  14. Place the a layer of pasta sheets.
  15. Repeat in the same order for 3 or 4 times.
  16. Enjoy your perfect layered Lasagna!
